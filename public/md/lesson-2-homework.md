#### Answer Key

**Translate the following sentences:**
1. Metella amīcum vocat. - Metella calls the friend
2. Dominus labōrat. - The master works
3. Servus est in atrio. - The servant is in the atrium.
4. amicus Metellam salutat. - The friend greets Metella
5. Ego sum mercator. - I am a merchant
6. mercātor pecūniam capit. - The merchant grabs the money.

**Label the case each word is in:**
1. Mercatori - nominative (2nd decl. Plural)
2. Dominum - accusative (2nd decl.)
3. Ancillae - dative (1st decl.)
4. Amicis - dative (2nd, plural)
5. togam - accusative (1st decl.)

**List the god/goddess**
1. God of fire - Vulcan
2. God of the underworld - pluto
3. Goddess of war - Bellona
4. King of the gods - Jupiter
5. God of the home - Vesta
6. Queen of the gods - Juno
7. God of war -Mars
8. God of wisdom - Minerva
