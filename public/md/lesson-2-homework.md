# Worksheet Lesson 2 Answer Key

## Translate the following sentences:
7. Metella amīcum vocat. - Metella calls the friend
8. Dominus labōrat. - The master works
9. Servus est in atrio. - The servant is in the atrium.
10. amicus Metellam salutat. - The friend greets Metella
11. Ego sum mercator. - I am a merchant
12. mercātor pecūniam capit. - The merchant grabs the money.

## Label the case each word is in:
6. Mercatori - nominative (2nd decl. Plural)
7. Dominum - accusative (2nd decl.)
8. Ancillae - dative (1st decl.)
9. Amicis - dative (2nd, plural)
10. togam - accusative (1st decl.)
