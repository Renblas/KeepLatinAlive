
### Lesson 5: Colors and Compound Verbs

#### Worksheet


**Section 1: Color Adjectives**  
Write the correct form of the color adjective.

1. tunica (fem. sg. acc.) + ruber → ________  
2. equus (masc. sg. nom.) + albus → ________  
3. rosae (fem. pl. nom.) + ruber → ________  

**Section 2: Compound Verbs**  
Give the meaning of the compound verb.

1. intrat (from in + eō) → ________  
2. adeunt (from ad + eō) → ________  
3. portat → importat → ________  

**Section 3: Translate to English**  
1. Puella tunicam rubram portat.  
2. Gladiātor arēnam intrat.  
3. Militēs oppidum adeunt.  

**Section 4: Translate to Latin**  
1. The soldier enters the red house.  
2. The girl carries a white toga.  

**Section 5: Culture – Roman Dining**  
List the three main daily Roman meals in order.

#### Answer Key (Separate)

**Section 1:**  
1. rubram  
2. albus  
3. rubrae  

**Section 2:**  
1. enters  
2. approach  
3. carries in  

**Section 3:**  
1. The girl carries/wears a red tunic.  
2. The gladiator enters the arena.  
3. The soldiers approach the town.  

**Section 4:**  
1. Mīles villam rubrum intrat.  
2. Puella togam albam portat.  

**Section 5:**  
ientāculum (breakfast), prandium (lunch), cēna (dinner)
