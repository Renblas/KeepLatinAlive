#### Worksheet


**Section 1: Perfect Tense Forms**

**Section 2: Adjective Agreement**  
Agree magnus, -a, -um.

2. Nautae portum vīdērunt.  
3. Servus dominum laudāvit.  

**Section 4: Translate to Latin (use perfect tense)**
